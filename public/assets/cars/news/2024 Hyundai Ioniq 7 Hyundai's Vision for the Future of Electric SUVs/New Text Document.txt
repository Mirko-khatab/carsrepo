Performance and Powertrain
Electric Global Modular Platform (E-GMP)
The Ioniq 7 is built on Hyundai's E-GMP platform, designed exclusively for electric vehicles.

Powertrain Options:
Single-Motor Rear-Wheel Drive: Estimated 308 horsepower.
Dual-Motor All-Wheel Drive: Estimated 483 horsepower.
Acceleration:
The dual-motor variant is expected to achieve 0-60 mph in under 5 seconds.
Driving Dynamics:
Features independent suspension and adjustable ride height for varied terrains.
Multiple driving modes including Eco, Normal, Sport, and Snow.
Towing Capacity
Capability: The Ioniq 7 offers a towing capacity of up to 5,000 lbs, suitable for trailers, boats, and recreational equipment.
Interior Luxury and Comfort
Spacious and Innovative Cabin
The Ioniq 7's interior is designed to be a comfortable and versatile space for passengers.

Seating:
Three-row seating accommodating up to seven passengers.
Configurable seating arrangements with swivel seats for the second row.
Materials:
Sustainable materials used throughout, including recycled plastics and eco-processed leather.
Ambient Lighting:
Customizable LED lighting with a range of colors to suit any mood.
Panoramic Sunroof:
A large sunroof extends over all three rows, enhancing the sense of space.

Charging and Range
Impressive Electric Range
Battery Capacity:
Expected to feature a 100 kWh battery pack.
Range:
Estimated to offer up to 300 miles on a single charge (EPA estimated).

Ultra-Fast Charging:
Supports up to 350 kW charging speed.
Can charge from 10% to 80% in approximately 20 minutes.
Vehicle-to-Load (V2L) Functionality:
Allows the Ioniq 7 to supply power to external devices, ideal for camping or emergency situations.
