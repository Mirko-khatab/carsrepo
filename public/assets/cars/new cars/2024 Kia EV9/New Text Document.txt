Design and Exterior Features
Striking and Futuristic Design
The 2024 Kia EV9 showcases a bold design that blends futuristic elements with practical SUV styling.

Front Fascia: Features Kia's new "Digital Tiger Face" with slim LED headlights and a unique light pattern.
Body Lines: Sharp angles and clean lines give the EV9 a robust and modern appearance.
Wheels: Equipped with aerodynamic 20-inch alloy wheels designed to enhance efficiency.
Roofline: Slightly sloped roof for improved aerodynamics without compromising interior space.

Performance and Powertrain
Electric Global Modular Platform (E-GMP)
The EV9 is built on Hyundai Motor Group's E-GMP platform, designed exclusively for electric vehicles.

Powertrain Options:
Standard Model: Single electric motor with rear-wheel drive, producing approximately 200 horsepower.
Long-Range Model: Dual electric motors with all-wheel drive, delivering around 400 horsepower.
Acceleration:
The dual-motor variant is expected to achieve 0-60 mph in under 6 seconds.
Driving Dynamics:
Features independent front and rear suspension for a comfortable and controlled ride.
Multiple driving modes including Eco, Normal, Sport, and Snow.
Towing Capacity
Capability: The EV9 offers a towing capacity of up to 5,000 lbs, suitable for towing boats, trailers, and more.
Interior Luxury and Comfort
Spacious and Versatile Cabin
The EV9 offers a roomy interior designed with comfort and convenience in mind.

Seating:
Three-row seating accommodating up to seven passengers.
Second-row seats that can swivel 180 degrees for a lounge-like experience.
Materials:
Sustainable materials used throughout, including recycled plastics and vegan leather options.
Ambient Lighting:
Customizable ambient lighting with multiple color options to suit your mood.
Panoramic Sunroof:
A large sunroof that extends over the first and second rows, enhancing the sense of space.
Technology and Infotainment
Advanced Infotainment System
The EV9 is equipped with Kia's latest technology features.

Displays:
Dual 12.3-inch screens serving as the digital instrument cluster and infotainment system.
An additional 5-inch screen for climate control settings.
Connectivity:
Wireless Apple CarPlay® and Android Auto™ integration.
Multiple USB-C ports and wireless charging pads.
Sound System:
Premium Bose® audio system with surround sound technology.
Innovative Features
Head-Up Display:
Projects essential information onto the windshield, reducing the need to look away from the road.
Over-the-Air Updates:
Software updates can be delivered remotely to keep the vehicle's systems current.
Remote Smart Parking Assist:
Allows the driver to park or retrieve the vehicle remotely using the key fob.
Safety and Driver Assistance
Kia Drive Wise Suite
The EV9 comes standard with a comprehensive suite of safety features.

Forward Collision-Avoidance Assist
Blind-Spot Collision-Avoidance Assist
Lane Keeping Assist and Lane Following Assist
Smart Cruise Control with Stop & Go
Highway Driving Assist 2:
Combines adaptive cruise control and lane-centering for semi-autonomous highway driving.
Surround View Monitor:
Provides a 360-degree view around the vehicle to assist with parking.
Structural Safety
Construction:
High-strength steel frame designed to absorb and distribute impact forces effectively.
Charging and Range
Impressive Electric Range
Battery Options:
Standard Battery: 77.4 kWh offering up to 250 miles of range.
Long-Range Battery: 100 kWh providing up to 300 miles of range.
Charging Capabilities:
DC Fast Charging:
Supports up to 350 kW charging speed.
Can charge from 10% to 80% in approximately 20 minutes.
Home Charging:
Compatible with Level 2 chargers up to 11 kW.
Vehicle-to-Load (V2L) Functionality
Feature:
Allows the EV9 to supply power to external devices or even charge another EV.
Ideal for camping or emergency power situations.
Price and Availability
Expected Pricing
Starting Price:
Base model is expected to start around $50,000.
Fully loaded models may reach up to $65,000.
Release Date
Availability:
The 2024 Kia EV9 is anticipated to arrive at dealerships in late 2023.
Pre-orders may open several months prior to the official release.
Warranty and Maintenance
Warranty:
5-year/60,000-mile limited basic warranty.
10-year/100,000-mile battery and powertrain warranty.
Maintenance:
Complimentary maintenance for the first three years or 36,000 miles.
